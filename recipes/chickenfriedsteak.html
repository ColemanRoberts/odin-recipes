Chicken Fried Steak

Description

Chicken fried steak is an inexpensive and usually tough cut of beef that has been tenderized, battered, and fried, in the manner fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs cube steak
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup frying oil (shortening, lard, or butter)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a skillet to medium heat and add 1/2 cup of frying oil.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking soda, and pepper in a shallow dish.
  3. Pour buttermilk in another shallow dish.
  4. Designate one hand your "dry hand" and the other your "wet hand".
  5. With your wet hand, place a piece of steak into the dish of flour. Then, with your dry hand, coat the piece on both sides with flour and place the coated piece into the buttermilk dish.
  6. With your wet hand, thoroughly dredge the piece in the buttermilk and then place the piece back into the flour dish.
  7. With your dry hand, coat the piece on both sides with flour again, and place the piece into the skillet.
  8. Cook until the edges become golden, about 1 1/2 - 2 minutes each side.
  9. Remove the piece from the skillet, and place on a paper towel lined plate to drain.
  10. Repeat from step 5 until each piece is cooked.
